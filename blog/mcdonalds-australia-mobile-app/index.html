McDonald's Australia Mobile App

November 2, 2012

It’s official(ly unofficial)! I just released the first, and quite soon after the second, version of McRosters. This simple little app allows McDonald’s staff to easily access their rosters on Android devices, and also has a few other handy little features built in as well.

Now noone has any excuse to be late, as you can have your phone automatically set shift reminders and add them to your devices calendar or any linked Google Calendar accounts.

You will also receive notifications of any unconfirmed SOCs in your MeTime inbox.

I still have a few more features yet, so stay tuned for more, but for now why not go and Download McRosters from Google Play Store!
