Access your Android contacts from your PC

August 12, 2012

The other day my phones battery went flat just as I was about to call a friend I had planned to meet up with. Much to my dismay, I only have said friends number stored on my phone (currently running Android ICS 4.0.4), however I had access to a landline phone and a PC connected to the world wide web.

To cut a long story short, if you have your Android device and contacts synced to your Google account, if you head on over to http://google.com/contacts you should be faced with all your contacts and an easy to use interface for editing, importing and exporting them.

This one saved my behind, perhaps it’ll do the same for you.
