From Gingerbread to Ice Cream Sandwich

August 6, 2012

Not a week ago I was trawling the web for info on how to jailbreak my Motorola xt910 razr phone in the hopes of upgrading Android from Gingerbread to ICS. I found a several how to guides, several ROMs and was pretty much set on getting on with it, however I got called into work before I started and since then haven’t given it another thought.

Then just moments ago, my phone sparked up with life and told me that an official ICS update from Motorola was available to download to my device.

So I as I sit here pondering whether this is really for the best, there are bits and bytes flying around my living room while the update downloads..

Perhaps I’ll be back with my thoughts on the update, perhaps not. I was simply excited that I don’t have to hack at and potentially brick my device now..
